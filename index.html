Project Idea

What is the project you are completing: The project that will be completed is an aim 1v1 game. This will allow people to verse their friends and compare aim. It will also allow them to practice certain parts of their game if they want to improve. The game will have multiple modes which you can select and each will represent a type of game setting; one mode will be for people who play first person shooters, and another mode will be for people who play in third person. The player can choose from different weapons which will put their aim to the test. There will also be a online mode which will show who has the best aim in their respective parts. This will have a leader board to encourage people to improve.

What is the reason for this project: Although there are many aim trainer games that show who is the best aimer, they are often too confusing and have many leaders’ boards as they focus on individual aspects of aiming. These games do not let people use the new skills in games, and are only useful for delveoping muscles memory. Having them train and also be able to compete against other players can help show that they have put their training to use. This will be used mostly by people who want to improve their aiming. It can help games who can’t/don’t have aim duels as the game will be able to mimic how the game is played so its just an external aim duel.

Detailed discription of the project: To start the game off the most important thing when it comes to an aiming game is the sensitivity of the mouse. That would be the target goal, I would like the game to be able to recognise the aiming multiplier of each different game. This would allow the user to choose the game and it will use that aim multiplier. Like a drop-down menu that will allow the user to search the game. This can then save them the trouble of trying to find a sensitivity multiplier which is never always accurate, ruining the whole point of the aim duel or aim trainer. The players need a type of map that they can challenge their friends at. The goal is to have at least 5 maps that the players can choose from. The map will be covered in high ground, low ground and boxes. Before the player joins the map, they can choose the weapon that they would like to use, every round they can have a chance to change their gun. When one player loses this is done by losing their health, the round ends. Another important aspect is having the player to choose the type of game that the aim duel will represent. This decides if the weapons accuracy changes by running/jumping or if the player needs to be viewed in a third person. This is because different games allow a different amount of running/jumping accuracy. For example, call of duty can allow you to run and jump which wont effect accuracy. However, a game like Counter strike global offensive requires you to be stationary. Like counter strike global offensive, Valorant is the same however it has a different period of break where you need to be stationary to have an accurate shot. Having multiple game settings will help the player perform to the best of their ability. Another feature is the aim training for people who want to improve their aim. This will consist of the player shooting AI that is scattered randomly in a select section in front of them. This helps them build muscle memory for aiming. Having a multiplayer game mode not only allows the player to aim duel their friends they can also test their aim against the rest of the region. This will be done by having a ranking system in online gameplay. It will be set to first to 10 rounds first player to those 10 rounds win. If the losing player, loses by a great margin of rounds, they will lose more ranked points. However, if the player loses by a fewer margin of rounds they will not lose as much ranked points. The ranked system will help to show who the best aimer is. The person who has the highest number of ranked points, will be classed the best aimer in that game mode. The player will be able to chose from a range of skins that they can look at whilst playing. These skins will be on their weapons as a vanity item, this will be one of the ways that the game will make money.

Tools required to complete this project: The tools required to make a game would either be Anvilnext or unreal engine. They are both known for making first person shooter games or third person shooters. Unreal engine was used in Fortnite and PUBG, whilst Anvilnext was used in making Rainbow Six siege. These are all very popular games that have had a successful career. I would also need a computer with enough space and processing power to create the game and refine it until it can be used on lower ended computers. This will let people who cannot afford a Computer with expensive parts to still be able to use the clients.

Skills required to complete this project: The skills that would be needed would be an advance knowledge of how to use Unreal engine or AnvilNext. An aim duel may be easy to create but importing aspects from different kind if games will take high level knowledge on the task. These skills can be found through a person who uses the client a lot or I can make plenty of games to gain the skills to be able to create the game. Some programming may be involved to allow the game to determine which game rules will be followed. Like the weapons that the player chooses, maps, trainers, etc.

What would the outcome be of the project: If the project is successful in its creation, people will be able to challenge their friends to see who has the better aim. After the game is created or whilst its nearing completion it will need to be advertised. The advertising will be the next challenge after the game is released. This is because the game may not as earn as much money as its aimed to be a free game. Skins for characters and weapons can be introduced to attempt gain money to pay for the advertisement. However, there is no guarantee that people would pay for the skins which would leave the team/me down in money.
